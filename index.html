About Rhetorix

Rhetorix was conceived to provide a dedicated platform where individuals can cultivate and showcase their public speaking, debating, and cultural co-curricular skills—areas often overshadowed by the predominant focus on sports in India. The initiative seeks to address this imbalance by fostering environments that encourage articulate expression, critical thinking, and leadership through verbal and cultural engagement.

Rhetorix's core mission is to transform the co-curricular landscape by creating structured opportunities for students to explore and develop these essential skills. By organizing debates, eloquence contests, and cultural forums, the platform aspires to nurture a generation capable of constructive dialogue and societal contribution through effective communication.

At its foundation, Rhetorix emphasizes inclusivity and accessibility, striving to empower voices from diverse backgrounds and encourage intellectual growth beyond conventional academic or athletic pursuits. This vision is guided by Harsh Bhardwaj, who established the organization in 2025 with a goal to democratize public speaking and cultural participation, ensuring these vital areas receive recognition and encouragement on par with other extracurricular fields.

Today, Rhetorix serves as a progressive platform dedicated to the sustained development of expressive and cultural competencies, reinforcing the belief that the power of speech and cultural participation are indispensable tools for individual and collective advancement.

Our Mission

At Rhetorix, our mission is to foster critical thinking, articulate expression, and informed discourse among the next generation of leaders.

Cultivate Critical Thinking

We challenge participants to analyze complex issues from multiple perspectives, developing their ability to think critically and make reasoned arguments.

Develop Oratory Skills

We provide a platform for debaters to hone their public speaking abilities, learning to articulate their thoughts clearly and persuasively.

Foster Diplomacy

Through committee-based debate, we teach the art of diplomacy—balancing firm convictions with respectful discourse and compromise.
